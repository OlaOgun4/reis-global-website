Visitor & Emergency Applications

Mobile and Web Operational Applications

Case Study

A family of solution concepts for secure visitor registration and emergency-response workflows.

MobileWeb DashboardIdentity CaptureWorkflowAudit
This public case study presents capability and architecture thinking at a safe level. Sensitive operational, security or client-confidential information is intentionally excluded.

The challenge

Reception and emergency-response processes are often paper-heavy, fragmented and difficult to monitor in real time.

REIS approach

Visitor management uses camera-assisted ID capture, structured registration, visit details, check-in/check-out and dashboards. Emergency concepts extend mobile reporting into dispatch and operational coordination.

Outcome

Digitised workflows with better auditability, visibility, operational insight and user experience.

REIS value demonstrated

The project demonstrates REIS capability to connect enterprise architecture, data, governance, integration and solution design around a complex operating problem rather than treating technology as a collection of isolated components.
